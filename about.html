About ELAN TRADE LINKS LLP

Building trusted partnerships in global petrochemical trading through integrity, quality, and excellence.

Company Introduction

A Premier Petrochemical Trading House

Founded in Dubai, UAE, ELAN TRADE LINKS LLP has established itself as a trusted name in the international petrochemical trading industry. We specialize in the sourcing, trading, and supply of a comprehensive range of petroleum products, hydrocarbons, and industrial chemicals.

Our strategic location in Dubai — one of the world's leading trading hubs — positions us at the crossroads of global commerce, enabling efficient access to markets across the Middle East, Africa, and Asia.

Who We Are

Industry Experts with Global Vision

We are a team of seasoned professionals with decades of combined experience in petrochemical trading, supply chain management, and international commerce. Our expertise spans across refining, manufacturing, logistics, and quality assurance.

At ELAN TRADE LINKS LLP, we believe in building long-term relationships founded on trust, transparency, and mutual success. Every transaction reflects our commitment to delivering exceptional value to our clients.
