About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Petrochemical Trading

ELAN TRADE LINKS LLP is a Dubai-based petrochemical trading company specializing in the sourcing, trading, and supply of premium petroleum products, hydrocarbons, and industrial chemicals across global markets.

With deep industry expertise and an extensive supplier network, we deliver reliable, quality-assured products to refineries, manufacturers, and distributors worldwide.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and trading process.

What products does ELAN TRADE LINKS LLP supply?

We supply elemental sulphur, petroleum products, hydrocarbons and solvents, oilfield solutions, and alcohols and glycols sourced from certified global suppliers.

Which regions do you serve?

We operate across the Middle East, Africa, South Asia, and Southeast Asia, with logistics support for international and domestic deliveries.

How can I request a product quote?

Submit an inquiry through our contact form or reach us directly by phone or email. Share product name, quantity, destination, and required specifications for a tailored quote.

Do you provide quality certificates with shipments?

Yes. We provide Certificates of Analysis (COA) and relevant compliance documentation with shipments to ensure product quality and traceability.

What is your typical delivery timeline?

Delivery timelines depend on product availability, origin, and destination. Our team coordinates logistics and keeps you updated from order confirmation to final delivery.
